## Design System
- **Colors**: Deep Islamic green (#1B4332), Rich gold (#D4AF37), Cream white (#FDF8F3)
- **Fonts**: Cairo (for headings & body text) - clean, modern Arabic font
- **RTL Layout**: Full right-to-left support throughout
- **Animations**: Scroll-triggered fade-ins, hover effects, neon pulse on AI section
- **Mobile-First**: Fully responsive from 320px to large screens
